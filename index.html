There Are Only 800 Trees Left in China

China has been thriving in the economy in the past few years.
But ever since the carbon emissions in China have increased,
many trees have been dying. Almost 4.5 billion trees have died
in China, and the tree population is still decreasing rapidly. A researcher
at The University of China has reported, "After exploring China, we have
realized that only 800 trees are remaining in the country." (Randy Jones).
People will have to leave China because there won't be enough trees,
said a reporter from CNN. Many residents of China have had to migrate to
another country forcefully by the government but were given compensation
to upwards of 50 thousand U.S. dollars. A former Chinese citizen
was interviewed and stated that he was, "Angry when I was given a notice
by government officials saying that he had to move to a different place."
